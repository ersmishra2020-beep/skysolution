Our Story

Built with Vision

SkyDevOps Solution was founded with a single, powerful vision: to make world-class DevOps and cloud automation accessible to businesses of every size across India and beyond.

From a one-person initiative by Ms. Sushila Mishra, SkyDevOps Solution has grown into a trusted IT services partner for clients across the education, healthcare, hospitality, and banking sectors.

We believe technology should work for people — not the other way around. That's why we build automation solutions that are practical, scalable, and tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Sushila Mishra

Sole Owner & CEO – SkyDevOps Solution

Ms. Sushila Mishra is the founder and sole owner of SkyDevOps Solution. With deep expertise in IT services, cloud technologies, and DevOps practices, she established the company with the goal of bridging the gap between businesses and modern technology.

Her passion for empowering young talent led to the creation of SkyDevOps Solution's structured internship program — which has already trained over 200 students with real-world skills and official certifications. Under her leadership, the company continues to grow as a trusted partner for clients across multiple industry sectors.

Our Journey

Milestones & Growth

2023 — Company Founded

SkyDevOps Solution established in Karnataka, India by Ms. Sushila Mishra with a focus on DevOps consulting and cloud services.

2023 — First Clients Onboarded

Successfully delivered CI/CD pipeline and cloud migration projects for initial clients in the education and healthcare sectors.

2024 — Internship Program Launched

Launched the structured 2-track internship training program, providing real-world DevOps experience to students across India.

2024 — 100+ Interns Trained

Reached the milestone of 100+ interns successfully trained and certified across both technical and business tracks.

2025 — Industry Expansion

Expanded automation solutions to banking and hospitality sectors, serving clients with tailored IT automation packages.

2025 — Growing Strong

50+ projects delivered, 200+ interns trained, and a growing portfolio of satisfied clients across 4 industry verticals.
