Internship Rules & Guidelines

All interns must read, understand, and agree to these rules before joining the program.

⚠️ These rules are mandatory. Violation of any rule may result in termination of internship and withholding of the completion certificate.

1. General Code of Conduct

  • Interns must maintain a high standard of professional behaviour at all times.
  • Respect all team members, mentors, employees, and clients of SkyDevOps Solution.
  • Use of abusive, offensive, or discriminatory language is strictly prohibited.
  • Interns must not indulge in any activity that damages the reputation of SkyDevOps Solution.
  • Any conflict with peers or mentors must be reported to the program coordinator immediately.
  • Interns are expected to maintain a positive, collaborative, and growth-oriented attitude.

2. Attendance & Availability

  • Interns must maintain a minimum of 80% attendance throughout the program.
  • Daily availability during designated working hours (10:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST) is mandatory for remote interns.
  • Absence of more than 2 consecutive days without prior notice will lead to a warning.
  • Leave must be applied at least 24 hours in advance unless it is a medical emergency.
  • Interns working remotely must be reachable on official communication channels during working hours.

3. Tasks, Assignments & Deadlines

  • All assigned tasks and projects must be completed on time with quality output.
  • Interns must submit weekly progress reports to their assigned mentor.
  • Plagiarism in any assignment, report, or code submission is strictly prohibited.
  • Requests for deadline extensions must be made at least 24 hours before the due date.
  • Interns are expected to ask questions and seek help proactively rather than submitting incomplete work.
  • Code must follow best practices, be properly documented, and reviewed before submission.

4. Confidentiality & Data Security

  • Interns must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before starting the program.
  • All client data, project details, and internal processes are strictly confidential.
  • Sharing any company or client information on social media, forums, or with third parties is prohibited.
  • Interns must not copy, retain, or distribute any proprietary code, data, or documentation.
  • All company-provided credentials, access tokens, and keys must be kept secure and not shared.
  • Any security incident must be reported to the supervisor immediately.

5. Communication & Tools

  • Official communication must happen only through company-approved channels (Email, Slack, or WhatsApp group).
  • Interns must respond to messages within 2 hours during working hours.
  • Professional language must be used in all official communication.
  • All project-related work must be tracked using the designated project management tool.
  • Personal social media must not be used for work-related communication.

6. Certificate & Completion Criteria

  • The internship completion certificate will be issued only upon fulfilling all program requirements.
  • Minimum 80% attendance is mandatory for certificate eligibility.
  • All assigned projects and tasks must be completed and approved by the mentor.
  • Interns must submit a final project report or presentation before the last day.
  • Interns who leave mid-program without valid reason will not receive a certificate.
  • Certificates are signed by Ms. Sushila Mishra, Sole Owner & CEO, SkyDevOps Solution.

7. Intellectual Property

  • All work, code, designs, and content created during the internship remain the property of SkyDevOps Solution.
  • Interns may include completed (non-confidential) projects in their personal portfolios with prior written permission.
  • Any invention or innovation during the internship must be disclosed to the company.

8. Termination Policy

  • SkyDevOps Solution reserves the right to terminate an internship at any time for misconduct.
  • Interns may voluntarily exit the program with a 7-day written notice to the program coordinator.
  • Grounds for immediate termination include: breach of NDA, plagiarism, harassment, or repeated violations.
  • No certificate will be issued in case of termination for misconduct.

9. Stipend & Compensation

  • The internship may be paid or unpaid depending on the track and batch — details will be mentioned in the offer letter.
  • Any agreed stipend will be credited on the last working day of each month.
  • Interns are not eligible for employee benefits, EPF, or insurance unless specifically mentioned.
By accepting the internship offer letter, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to all the above rules and guidelines set by SkyDevOps Solution under the authority of Ms. Sushila Mishra, Sole Owner & CEO.
